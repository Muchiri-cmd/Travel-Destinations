Top 5 Dream Travel Destinations

1. Brazil

Land of the Palms

Why Brazil?

The culture at Brazil just seems an interesting one, portraying joy and pure vibes just like their playing style on soccer pitches.

Things I can do there:

  • Play street soccer
  • Visit Christ the Redeemer statue
  • Visit the favelas
  • Watch Neymar play at Santos

2. Spain

The Bull's Skin

Why Spain?

It gives royalty vibes, a rich culture and also has pretty dope architecture aesthetics.

Things I can do there:

  • Visit Museums
  • Watch a Barca game at the Camp Nou
  • Attend a Real Madrid game at the Santiago Bernabeu

3. Dubai

The City of Gold

Why Dubai?

Well we all want to go to Dubai, LOL. It just seems a nice place to be especially with the beautiful scenery and wealthy atmosphere.

Things I can do there:

  • Visit the Burj Khalifa
  • Do quad bikes in the desert
  • Attend a yacht party
  • Rent a Ferrari

4. Switzerland

Land of Milk and Honey

Why Switzerland?

It just gives off calm, peaceful vibes with all that fresh air, pretty mountain views and just pure nature beauty.

Things I can do there:

  • Take a scenic train ride through the Alps
  • Try out skiing in the Swiss mountains
  • Visit the famous Lake Geneva
  • Get a Swiss watch
  • Eat loads of Swiss chocolate

5. South Africa

Rainbow Nation

Why South Africa?

South Africa just sounds like such a vibe. The mix of culture, wildlife, and amazing landscapes makes it feel like the kind of place you just have to experience at least once.

Things I can do there:

  • Go on a safari
  • Visit Table Mountain in Cape Town
  • Chill at the beaches in Durban
  • Explore local markets and try South African food
